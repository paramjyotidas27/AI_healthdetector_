About the Project

The AI Health Detector is an initiative designed to bridge the gap between health awareness and technology. It aims to empower users with accessible knowledge about diseases — especially Sexually Transmitted Diseases (STDs) and other common infections — in an easy, informative, and interactive way. This project helps individuals make more informed decisions about their well-being through AI-supported awareness tools and health questionnaires.

Our objective is to educate, prevent, and protect. By providing simplified, verified information and smart detection interfaces, we hope to reduce misinformation, increase early detection awareness, and build a digitally conscious health community.

Together, let's create a world where knowledge and awareness lead to healthier choices and a safer tomorrow.
